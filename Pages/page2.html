About Us

About The Furry Hotel

The Furry Hotel is a start-up pet day camp, founded in 2018 in Atlanta, GA. We are known for our outstanding pet care center, offering top quality daycare, boarding, and also mobile services. At The Furry Hotel, we always strive to provide supreme attentiveness to the needs of your furry loved ones.
We offer care for all different types of pets, ranging from dogs, cats, puppies and kittens, and even care for unique and exotic pets.

Our Vision

Here at the Furry Hotel, we have a vision to provide the most watchful care for your furry friends as possible. We know that your pets are a part of your family, and you can be ensured that they will receive loving care, like they would at home.
